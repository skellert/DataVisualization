Project Dataset

  • Source of Dataset
  • This is a dataset from Data.gov and is therefore open for use.
  • The data set has a mix of numerical and categorical information. The numerical information is primarily death counts broken out by a wide number of categories. These categories include county, state, age band, race, and type of death.
  • I spent a long time looking through the datasets on Data.gov for one that had a good mix of interesting categorical and numeric information. This dataset has a wealth of both and also has a lot of potential for interesting discoveries. I hope to be able to find geographic patterns in the data as well as trends between the numerous other categories such as race, age band, and type of death.
